Main Courses

Our main courses are crafted to satisfy every appetite with bold flavors and fresh ingredients.
From hearty meat dishes to flavorful vegetarian options, there's something for everyone.
Indulge in classics like our Grilled Ribeye Steak, enjoy the zesty flavors of Pan-Seared Chicken Piccata,
or savor the freshness of our Honey-Glazed Salmon.
Each dish is prepared with care to ensure every bite is a delightful experience. Explore the menu and find your new favorite!


  • Grilled Ribeye Steak
    Juicy, tender ribeye steak grilled to perfection, seasoned with garlic and rosemary, and served with a side of roasted vegetables.

  • Pan-Seared Chicken Piccata
    Tender chicken breast cooked in a lemon-butter caper sauce, served over a bed of angel hair pasta.

  • Honey-Glazed Salmon
    Fresh Atlantic salmon fillet glazed with honey and soy sauce, accompanied by garlic mashed potatoes and steamed asparagus.
